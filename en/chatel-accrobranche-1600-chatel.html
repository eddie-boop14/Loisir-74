What is Châtel Accrobranche 1600

The Châtel Accrobranche 1600 (marketed as Châtel Aventures) is a treetop adventure park set in natural forest at the top of the Super-Châtel cable car, at 1,600 m altitude, in the heart of the Portes du Soleil ski area. Open in summer, it offers 7 all-natural courses exploiting the high-altitude forest: zip lines, monkey bridges, vines, nets, pendulum jumps, all under instructor supervision.

The courses are graded for every age and size: P'tit Kid for children from age 2 and up to 1.05 m (with an adult), 2 Kid courses for 1.05–1.35 m, and 4 Super courses from 1.35 m (or 1.50 m without adult). All jumps, bridges and zip lines use a continuous lifeline system — fully securing progress, a real advantage for younger climbers.

Access via the Super-Châtel cable car (foot passenger ticket included with the activity in many packages). Summer 2026 season, daily during school holidays.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

Châtel Accrobranche 1600 (Châtel Aventures) is a tree-top adventure park at 1,600 m altitude in Châtel, in the Portes du Soleil. 7 courses from age 2, Quick Jump (free-fall), access by the Super-Châtel gondola.

What is the minimum age at Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

Châtel Accrobranche 1600 welcomes children from age 2 (up to 1.05 m tall, accompanied by an adult) on the P'tit Kid Park. More committed courses are available from 1.05 m and 1.35 m.

What are the prices at Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

2025 individual prices at Châtel Accrobranche 1600 (to be confirmed for 2026): P'tit Kid Park €14.90, Kid Park €22.50, Super Park €31.20. Gondola, gear and activity are included. Verified on 10 June 2026.

What are the opening times of Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

Châtel Accrobranche 1600 is open every day from 4 July to 30 August 2026. Last admission at 3 pm for 2 h, at 4 pm for 1 h. Verified on chatel.com on 10 June 2026.

What is the Quick Jump at Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

The Quick Jump at Châtel Accrobranche 1600 is a controlled free-fall experience from over 10 m — a strong thrill, extra to the tree-top ticket.

How do you get to Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

Access to Châtel Accrobranche 1600 is by the Super-Châtel gondola in Châtel village (74390). The few-minute ride is included in the tree-top ticket.

Do you need to book at Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

Booking is advised at Châtel Accrobranche 1600, especially in peak season (July–August). Bookings on chatel.com or chatelreservation.com.

What gear do you need for Châtel Accrobranche 1600?

For Châtel Accrobranche 1600, plan closed shoes (trainers), sportswear, long hair tied back. Helmets, harnesses and lanyards are supplied by the park.
