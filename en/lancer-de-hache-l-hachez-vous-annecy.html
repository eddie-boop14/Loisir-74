What is L'Hachez-Vous Annecy

L'Hachez-Vous Annecy is the first axe-throwing centre in the Annecy area, located in Annecy, Haute-Savoie. The venue offers 7 individual lanes, allowing up to 21 people to play simultaneously, in the warm atmosphere of a games bar. Each session starts with a safety briefing led by a qualified coach who teaches the proper technique (grip, stance, rotational or no-spin throw), before moving on to various games and challenges to make the experience accessible for beginners and seasoned throwers alike.

Who is it for? The activity is open from age 14 and is a hit with groups of friends, stag/hen parties, teen and adult birthdays, corporate team-building events or couples looking for an original night out. The centre offers coached sessions of about 1h-1h30, lane privatisation and full birthday packages with cake and drinks. The venue also features a full games bar — billiards, foosball, electronic darts, board games — and a selection of local craft beers to extend the evening after your session.

Booking and access. Booking is strongly recommended, especially weekends and evenings: it can be done online on lancerdehache-annecy.fr or by phone. Prices vary by package (single session, birthday, stag/hen, privatisation) and generally run around €20-30 per person for a session. The activity is indoor, open year-round and ideal in rainy weather or for evenings. Comfortable clothes and closed shoes recommended; the coaches provide all equipment and ensure safety at all times.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

From what age can you throw an axe in Annecy?

The activity is open from age 14 at L'Hachez-Vous Annecy. Minors must be accompanied or have parental authorisation per the centre's conditions.

How much does a session cost?

A coached session usually costs €20-30 per person depending on the package (single session, birthday, stag/hen, privatisation). Exact 2026 prices to confirm on lancerdehache-annecy.fr.

How long is a session?

A standard session lasts about 1h to 1h30, including safety briefing and learning. Plan a little more for group packages with tournament.

Do I need to book?

Yes, booking is strongly recommended, especially Friday/Saturday evenings, Sundays and school holidays. Book online or by phone.

Is the activity dangerous?

No, as long as the coach's instructions are followed. The centre is designed with netting, regulation targets and systematic briefings. The coach is present throughout.

Can the venue be privatised?

Yes, full or partial privatisation packages exist for stag/hen parties, birthdays and businesses. Request a quote directly from the centre.

What to do after the session?

The games bar (pool, foosball, darts, board games) and the craft-beer selection make it easy to extend the evening on site.
