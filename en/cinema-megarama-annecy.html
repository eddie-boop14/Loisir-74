What is Megarama Annecy

Megarama Annecy is a cinema located in Annecy, Haute-Savoie. Megarama Annecy is the other major multiplex in Annecy, operated under the national Megarama brand. Several auditoriums equipped with digital laser projection and Dolby sound (Atmos in some). The venue offers a weekly programme rotating with national releases (dramas, comedies, blockbusters), art-house films in original version (OV) or dubbed (VF) depending on the showing, occasional previews and themed events (festivals, talks with directors, family screenings, opera and concert relays).

Character and atmosphere. A city-centre cinema, Megarama Annecy attracts both local and visiting audiences for national and international releases, in VF or OV with subtitles. Comfortable seats, quality sound and digital HD/laser projection ensure a modern experience. Ticketing typically includes full price, reduced rate (students, under 14/18, jobseekers), the French youth Pass Culture (ages 15-20), morning shows and a season pass (5- or 10-ticket booklet) to lower the per-seat cost.

Practical info and access. Located in Annecy, the cinema is accessible by car (public car parks nearby), bus (Sibra lines), bike (cycle paths) or on foot from the centre. Tickets are available on site and online (https://www.megarama.fr/annecy or partner platforms such as AlloCiné) for quick booking. Wheelchair-accessible auditoriums, concession stand on site. The programme changes every week — check the official site or AlloCiné for current films and showtimes.

When to visit

When to visit

All year round — weekly programme refreshed on Wednesday. Busier in the evening (6 pm-10 pm), on weekends and around event releases. Morning and late-afternoon sessions are quietest. Winter: ideal back-up when weather closes the slopes.
Events : The cinema joins national operations (Fête du Cinéma in late June/early July, Printemps du Cinéma in March, Rentrée du Cinéma in September) and hosts occasional previews, talks with directors and relays. Programming changes every Wednesday — see the official site.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What films are showing at Megarama Annecy this week?

The schedule at Megarama Annecy changes every week, usually on Wednesday. Check the official site (https://www.megarama.fr/annecy) or AlloCiné for the weekly schedule.

How do I buy a ticket for Megarama Annecy?

You can buy your ticket at the box office, online on the official website (https://www.megarama.fr/annecy) or via a partner platform (AlloCiné). Pickup at the kiosk or e-ticket on smartphone.

What are the ticket prices at Megarama Annecy?

Prices include full rate (~€9-12), reduced (students, under 14/18, jobseekers), Pass Culture (15-20), morning shows and season passes. Exact 2026 prices are on the official site or on the box-office price board.

Are films shown in OV or VF at Megarama Annecy?

It depends on the showing. Mainstream releases are shown in VF (French dubbed). Art-house, classic or festival films may be shown in OV (original version) with subtitles. The format is shown on the ticket booking page.

Is there parking at Megarama Annecy?

The cinema is in central Annecy: plan for a nearby public car park (Bonlieu, Hôtel de Ville, Courier). Public transport (Sibra) or the cycle greenway is also a good option.

Is Megarama Annecy accessible to people with reduced mobility?

Yes, the auditoriums are fitted for visitors with reduced mobility (dedicated spaces, level access or lifts, accessible toilets). To confirm for a specific showing, contact the cinema.

Does the cinema run family screenings?

Yes, the cinema regularly programmes family-friendly films (Wednesday, Saturday, school holidays) and specific operations (Ciné-goûter, École et cinéma, holiday activities). The family programme is published weekly.
