Haute-Savoie offers a great deal without a ticket: public beaches on Lac d'Annecy and Léman, waterfalls, viewpoints, trails, villages and free-entry heritage. This page lists sites whose free entry is verified on the fiche — not disguised "free except parking" entries: when a side cost exists, the fiche says so. A paid site, or one whose status is unverified, is not listed here.

Frequently asked questions

Does "free" really mean no cost at all?

Free here means: free entry, verified on the fiche. Side costs may exist (parking, access cable car, rentals) — when they do, the site's fiche says so. Check it before you go.

Can free entry change with the season?

Yes, some beaches or sites charge in high season. The fiche states the known conditions and their verification date — when in doubt, the official source linked on the fiche is authoritative.
