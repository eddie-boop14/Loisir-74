Easy hikes around Lake Annecy (under 2.5 hours)

Walking above the lake without chasing a summit: trails, viewpoints and loops whose verified duration on the sheet stays under 2.5 hours. The criterion is a fact, not a feeling of easiness.

How this selection is made — Entry criterion: Annecy-basin trail whose stated sheet duration is ≤ 2.5 h. Trails without a verified duration are excluded (honest by default). Updates automatically.
