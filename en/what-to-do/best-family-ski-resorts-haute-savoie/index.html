The best family ski resorts in Haute-Savoie

Twenty-eight resorts on our sheets, all documented from official sources: the ones here are flagged family-friendly — gentle ski areas, living villages, real off-slope life. The ranking is computed, not decreed.

How this selection is made — Entry criterion: resort flagged family-friendly on its sheet. Ranking: real photo, richness of verified facts (ski area, altitude, access, off-slope). No editorial opinion. Updates automatically.

Avoriaz

Morzine

Avoriaz, a 100% pedestrian resort at 1 800 m at the heart of the Portes du Soleil: ski-in ski-out, snowparks, Aquariaz and a renowned family-friendly and freestyle terrain.

Bernex

Bernex

Bernex, a family-friendly village resort in the Pays d'Évian at the foot of the Dent d'Oche: alpine and Nordic skiing, a real Chablais balcony over Lake Léman. Ski area, access and off-the-slopes activities.

Chamonix-Mont-Blanc

Chamonix-Mont-Blanc

Chamonix-Mont-Blanc, a multi-sector valley at the foot of Mont-Blanc: Brévent-Flégère, Grands Montets, Le Tour-Balme, Les Houches, from 1035 to 3300 m. Ski areas, access and practical information.

Châtel

Châtel

Châtel, a village resort in the Portes du Soleil on the French-Swiss border, linked to Morgins: 120 km of pistes, cross-country skiing, the Linga snowpark and an international ski area. Access and information.

Combloux

Combloux

Combloux, a family village in Haute-Savoie facing Mont-Blanc: the Les Portes du Mont-Blanc ski area linked to Évasion Mont-Blanc, a biotope and a baroque church.

Cordon

Cordon, a balcony village facing the Mont-Blanc range above Sallanches: a small family ski area (11 km of pistes, 1 000-1 600 m), a Baroque village and a head-on view of the massif.

Flaine, a modernist mountain village (Marcel Breuer) at the heart of the Grand Massif: 1 600-2 500 m, 64 pistes, ski-in/ski-out, open-air art. Access, ski area and info.

Bellevaux

Hirmentaz (Bellevaux) and Les Habères (Habère-Poche), two family-friendly resorts in the Vallée Verte with linked ski areas: 43 pistes, cross-country skiing, 30 km from Genève.
