The best Mont-Blanc viewpoints — verified sightlines

Actually seeing Mont-Blanc: every place below carries a view of the massif verified against an official source (orientation table, operator, tourist office) — that is the entry criterion, not an impression. Montenvers, for instance, is absent: its operator states Mont-Blanc cannot be seen from there.

How this selection is made — Entry criterion: Mont-Blanc view verified against an official source (the sheet's 'panorama' fact). Ranking: real photo, free access, fact richness. Updates automatically.
