The best waterfalls in Haute-Savoie, ranked openly

From the Rouget, 'queen of the Alps', to the Chablais' hidden gorges: Haute-Savoie is waterfall country. This ranking is not an opinion — it is computed from our sheets: access, season, real photos, richness of verified facts.

How this selection is made — 'Best' = computed ranking: real photo, free access, richness of verified facts (access, season, duration). No editorial opinion. Updates automatically as sheets evolve.
