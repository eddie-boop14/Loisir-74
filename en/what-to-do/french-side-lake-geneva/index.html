Lake Geneva, French side: Thonon to Yvoire

Between Geneva and the Swiss shore opposite, the French side of Lake Geneva lines up medieval towns, thermal baths, beaches and harbours. Compiled from our shoreline sheets — Yvoire to Meillerie.
