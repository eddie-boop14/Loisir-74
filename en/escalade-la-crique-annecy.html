La Crique combines Criq'Bloc, an indoor bouldering gym, and Criq'Parc, an outdoor climbing park in Saint-Jorioz: bouldering, routes, slackline, coaching and Lake Annecy vibes.

What is La Crique — Criq'Bloc & Criq'Parc

La Crique is a unique climbing site in Saint-Jorioz, on the west shore of Lake Annecy, combining two complementary worlds: Criq'Bloc, an indoor bouldering gym of around 800 m² with regularly refreshed circuits, and Criq'Parc, an outdoor climbing park unique in Haute-Savoie that combines outdoor lead routes on poles, slackline workshops, ziplines and pole-based fun zones — all in a stunning natural setting near the lake.

Who is it for? La Crique welcomes all levels, from families with kids to experienced climbers. Group classes, school-holiday courses for kids/teens, birthday packages (from age 6-7) and team-building outings are on offer. Gear is rented on site (harness, shoes, helmet for the outdoor park). For the park, a safety briefing is mandatory before accessing the workshops.

Prices and opening hours. Criq'Bloc adult entry is around €12-15 at full rate, while Criq'Parc access is around €15-22 depending on the package (park only, park + bouldering, half-day). The bouldering gym is open year-round (extended evening/weekend hours); the outdoor park runs from April to October, mainly afternoons, weekends and school holidays. Booking recommended in high season.

How to get there

By car

From Annecy: ~15-20 min via the D1508 along the west shore of the lake to Saint-Jorioz. From Geneva (~55 km): A41 then D1508, ~50 min. Free on-site parking.

Public transport

Sibra bus or Y'a Plus Q' (lake network) between Annecy and Saint-Jorioz. Stop close to the site.

By bike

Lake Annecy greenway directly linked to Saint-Jorioz from Annecy (~15 km, flat). Bike parking on site.

When to visit

When to visit

Criq'Bloc year-round — ideal in bad weather. Criq'Parc April to October — best between May and September in dry weather. Peaks: Wednesday, weekends, school holidays.
Events : School-holiday courses, themed gym nights, festive park events in summer (drinks, end-of-season parties). Schedule on the website and La Crique social media.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Difference between Criq'Bloc and Criq'Parc?

Criq'Bloc is the indoor bouldering gym, open year-round. Criq'Parc is the outdoor climbing park (pole routes, slackline, ziplines) open April-October. Both are on the same site.

Minimum age?

The outdoor park welcomes kids from around age 5-6 (depending on the workshop), bouldering from age 4 with family. Classes from age 6-7.

Do I need to book?

Not required for open bouldering. Recommended for the park in high season, classes, courses and birthdays.

How much is entry?

Bouldering adult ~€12-15, park ~€15-22 depending on package (park only, park + bouldering, half-day). 2026 prices on annecy-lacrique.com.

Do I need to be experienced for the park?

No, the park is accessible to beginner families after a safety briefing. Outdoor routes are equipped with top-rope.

What if it rains?

Criq'Bloc stays open indoors. Criq'Parc closes in bad weather (safety). Check by phone beforehand.

Parking and cafeteria?

Yes, free on-site parking and cafeteria/terrace area to refuel between sessions, with park views.
