Frequently asked questions

Which outings to do with children in Haute-Savoie?

The wildlife and theme parks (Grand Parc d'Andilly, Tropicaland, Grande Jeanne wildlife park), treetop adventure from age 3-4, and indoor leisure parks such as Vitam or Léman Kid.

Which outings are stroller-accessible?

Several parks (Grand Parc d'Andilly, Vitam, Île de Tortuga, base du Vuaz) allow strollers on their paths; treetop adventure, up in the trees, does not. Stroller access is stated on each listing.

What to do with children when it rains?

The indoor leisure parks — Vitam (aquatic area), Léman Kid, Île de Tortuga, C5 Kids Party and the Elevation Indoor trampoline park — are perfect in bad weather.

From what age can children do treetop adventure?

Most adventure parks offer children's courses from age 3-4 (sometimes up in the trees from age 2), with progressive levels. Check the minimum age and height on each listing.
