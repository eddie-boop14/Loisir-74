Swimming & watersports

Pools, slides, paddle and sailing — every way to get into the water.

Beyond the beaches, Haute-Savoie multiplies the ways to get into the water: water parks with slides, indoor pools, sailing schools, a wakepark and cruises on the lakes.

Read more

For swimming and play, water parks and aquatic centres run in summer and winter alike, indoors or outdoors.

For glide sports, watersports bases, sailing schools and a wakepark liven up Lac d'Annecy and Léman; cruises offer a more relaxed discovery.

Opening times and fares are highly seasonal. Each listing specifies the access, the commune and an official link.

On Lac d'Annecy as on Léman, hire and lessons are available by the hour or half-day.

Frequently asked questions

Where to swim indoors in Haute-Savoie?
Water parks and aquatic centres offer covered pools and slides, open all year.
Can you sail on Lac d'Annecy?
Yes, several watersports bases and schools offer sailing, paddle and windsurfing on Lac d'Annecy and Léman, to hire or as lessons.
What is a wakepark?
A body of water fitted with a cable that tows the rider (wakeboard, water-ski) without a boat; there are some on Lac d'Annecy and in the Genevois.
Where to take a cruise?
On Lac d'Annecy and Léman, companies offer commented cruises, meals on board and shuttles between the villages.
Which water park in Haute-Savoie?
Several fun aquatic centres with slides and children's areas are spread across the department, including in the resorts.
