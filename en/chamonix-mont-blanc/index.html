Chamonix-Mont-Blanc

17 leisure spots in Chamonix-Mont-Blanc · Haute-Savoie

At the foot of the Mont-Blanc, Chamonix-Mont-Blanc is the cradle of mountaineering and one of the most famous resorts in the Alps. The Aiguille du Midi cable car, the Montenvers train to the Mer de Glace, high-altitude lakes and mountain museums sit side by side.
