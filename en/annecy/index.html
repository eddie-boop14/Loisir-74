Annecy

40 leisure spots in Annecy · Haute-Savoie

Capital of Haute-Savoie, Annecy stretches along the Lac d'Annecy at the foot of the Bornes and the Semnoz. Nicknamed the “Venice of the Alps” for its canals and the Palais de l'Île, the town packs in the department's highest density of leisure: museums, beaches, cruises, parks, escape games and open-air activities, from Mont Veyrier to the lakeside Voie verte.
