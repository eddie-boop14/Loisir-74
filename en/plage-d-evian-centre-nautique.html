What is Plage du Centre Nautique d'Évian

The Plage du Centre Nautique d'Évian stretches over 3.5 hectares on the shore of Lake Geneva in Évian-les-Bains, in the historic Parc Dollfus. The complex combines a 50 m Olympic pool, a leisure pool, a paddling pool, a 160 m water slide (one of the longest in France) and direct access to the lake with diving boards.

The site has held the Blue Flag label since 2020 and has renewed it in the 2026 Teragir ranking, a sixth consecutive year. Recently refurbished, with modernised pools, diving boards, changing rooms and snack-bars, it is one of the flagship beach facilities of the French shore of Lake Geneva.

Lifeguards 2 May-21 September 2026. 2026 summer prices: adult around 5.50 EUR (2025 rate, 2026 to be confirmed), child reduced 3-10 EUR. Blue Flag 2026: yes. Step-free access (Tiralo available).
