Wheelchair and stroller access: what each fiche documents

Accessibility cannot be guessed: an "easy" trail may hide a section impassable by wheelchair, an equipped beach may only be equipped in summer. This page gathers, site by site, the wheelchair and stroller access information exactly as it appears on each fiche — the exact text, which sometimes states limits or conditions. Read each entry in full: a site's presence here means information exists, not that access is guaranteed.

140 of 425 sites document wheelchair access on their fiche — the others read "Not specified".

Frequently asked questions

Is a site listed here necessarily wheelchair accessible?

No. A site is listed because its fiche documents access information — which may describe partial, seasonal or limited access. The fiche's exact text is authoritative, not presence in this list.

How can I report an accessibility error?

Every fiche carries a "Report an issue" link. A correction verified on site or with the operator is added to the fiche after source checking.
