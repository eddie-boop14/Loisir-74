What is Paintball Zone 74

Paintball Zone 74 is a paintball venue located in Perrignier, Haute-Savoie. The venue is located in Perrignier (74550), in the Lower Chablais, 10 minutes from Thonon-les-Bains and 15 minutes from Lake Geneva, on a large field of several hectares. The site offers games on specially designed fields, with varied scenarios and full equipment provided (full face mask, marker, jumpsuit or chest protector, paintballs). Professional referees brief safety rules before each game.

Game formats. Several themed outdoor fields (forest, village, bunkers), full equipment and professional refs for dynamic scenario games. Usual scenarios include capture the flag, elimination, zone defence, protect the president, base attack/defence. A typical game lasts 10-15 minutes followed by a debrief, with several games totalling 1h30 to 2h of actual play. Pricing is generally degressive depending on number of paintballs (packages of 100, 200, 300 balls) and players.

Audience and conditions. Open to adults (16-18 depending on venue, sometimes with parental consent) and to children from age 8 in junior / low impact format (adapted markers, softer balls, reduced intensity). Very popular for birthdays, hen/stag parties and team building. Booking required with a minimum number of players (often 6-8). Wear sturdy covering clothes and sport or cleated shoes. Some paint splatter is unavoidable — bring a change of clothes.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

From what age can people play paintball at Paintball Zone 74?

Adult format is generally for ages 16+ (sometimes with parental consent between 16 and 18). A junior / low-impact format is offered from age 8 with adapted equipment and softer paintballs.

How much does a game cost at Paintball Zone 74?

Expect ~€25-35 per person for a 100-paintball package (equipment + 1h30 play) and ~€35-50 for 200 balls. Reduced rates above 8-10 players and for birthday/hen/stag packages. 2026 prices to be confirmed.

Do I need to book at Paintball Zone 74?

Yes, booking is required. The centre works in time slots with a minimum number of players (often 6-8). Booking by phone or via the official website. Confirmation advised 48h in advance.

What should I bring / wear at Paintball Zone 74?

Wear sturdy covering clothes with long sleeves (jeans or sports trousers, sweater) and sport or cleated shoes. The centre provides mask, jumpsuit/chest protector and marker. Bring a change of clothes and a towel.

Is paintball at Paintball Zone 74 indoor or outdoor?

See the entry: some venues play only outdoors (forest, themed fields), others offer an indoor CQB hall for short, fast games, sometimes both. Weather only affects outdoor fields.

How long does a session at Paintball Zone 74 take?

Plan 2-3h on site: safety briefing, kitting up, several 10-15 min games with debriefs, totalling 1h30-2h of actual play. Half a day for groups or birthdays.

Is paintball at Paintball Zone 74 dangerous or painful?

Well supervised, paintball is safe: compulsory mask, calibrated marker, initial briefing, referees on field. An impact can leave a small temporary bruise — that is expected. The junior / low-impact format strongly reduces intensity for children.
