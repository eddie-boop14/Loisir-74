Our method, in ten lines.

Loisirs 74 exists because there was no single place to find accurate info on public leisure sites in Haute-Savoie. Town-hall PDFs never surface on Google. Tourist-office pages all look alike. Generalist blogs copy what they find — often badly, often stale.

  • At least three official sources per site — municipality, tourist office, ONF, Natura 2000, data.gouv.
  • Blogs, Visorando, Komoot, TripAdvisor are not sources.
  • If a fact isn't confirmed anywhere, it doesn't appear. Never a "generally".
  • GPS checked on OpenStreetMap. Publication and update dates visible on every page.
