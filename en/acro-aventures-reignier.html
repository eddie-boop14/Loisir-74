What is Acro'Aventures Reignier

Acro'Aventures Reignier is an adventure park located in the Rocailles forest at Reignier-Ésery, 10 km from Annemasse, 20 km from Geneva and 20 km from La Roche-sur-Foron. Opened in 2016, it offers acrobatic courses at height in the trees and an Acro'Net, a suspended play structure in netting 12 meters high. The site benefits from a preserved natural forest setting, ideal for families and groups looking for a safe and fun outdoor activity in the French Geneva area.

The park offers four types of courses suited to all sizes and ages: the Grand Course (from 1m35, approximately 2h30) with Tarzan swing and return zip-lines of 200 m; the Junior Course (from 1m20, approximately 2h) with 5 loops including a zip-line course; the Children's Course (from 1 m, approximately 1h) with 4 loops; and the Acro'Net (all ages, 1 to 2h) with giant balls, ball pit, trampolines and slide. All courses are equipped with a continuous safety line system developed by Koala Equipment and designed by Altus, guaranteeing maximum safety while allowing autonomous progression once the briefing is completed.

The site also offers an orienteering course (from age 7, search for 20 markers in the forest) and a treasure hunt (from age 3) for the youngest. Acro'Aventures Reignier welcomes families, school groups, corporate committees, birthday parties, bachelor and bachelorette parties with adapted packages. Sportswear and closed shoes covering heel and toes are mandatory for safety reasons. Online booking is required on the official website, with the possibility to book until the last minute. The park stands out for its excellent value for money and professional on-site supervision.

How to get there

By car

From Annemasse (10 km, 15 min), follow D1206 towards La Roche-sur-Foron then Reignier exit, follow Route des Rocailles. From Geneva (20 km, 25 min), take A40 exit 13 Annemasse then D1206. From La Roche-sur-Foron (20 km), follow D1206 towards Annemasse then Reignier exit. A40/A410 interchange 5 km away. Free parking on site.

Public transport

Reignier SNCF train station on the Aix-les-Bains–Annemasse line, then 3 km by taxi or bike (no direct bus line). From Geneva, Léman Express to Annemasse then connection.

When to visit

When to visit

The park is open year-round with hours varying by season. High season (April to August) offers the widest time slots with evening openings until 7:30 pm on weekends. In winter, the park operates Wednesdays and weekends with daily opening during school holidays. Spring and autumn are ideal periods to enjoy the forest with less crowding. Allow 1 to 2h30 depending on the course chosen. Avoid heavy rain or strong wind days. Online booking required, possible until the last minute.
Events : Themed treasure hunts during season: Easter, summer classic, Halloween and Christmas. Children's birthday packages year-round by booking. Nighttime events and Acro'Athlon according to schedule (consult official website).

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I book Acro'Aventures Reignier?

Booking at Acro'Aventures Reignier is mandatory and done exclusively online at acro-aventures.com/reignier/. You can book until the last minute. By phone at 06 43 16 27 59, leave an SMS if voicemail.

What is the price for Acro'Aventures Reignier?

The price for Acro'Aventures Reignier varies by course: Grand Course adult (16+) €26, youth (under 16) €21, Junior Course €20, Children's Course €16, Acro'Net €16, Orienteering course €6. Group rate from 8 people. Verified May 14, 2026.

What are the hours for Acro'Aventures Reignier?

Acro'Aventures Reignier is open year-round with varying hours. In winter (Dec–Mar): Wed/Sat/Sun 1 pm–5 pm, school holidays daily 11 am–5 pm. In season (Apr–Aug): Mon–Fri 1 pm–5:30 pm, weekends 10 am–7:30 pm. Verified on official website May 14, 2026 — subject to seasonal changes.

From what age can you do Acro'Aventures Reignier?

Acro'Aventures Reignier is accessible from 1 m tall (approximately age 3) for the Children's Course and Acro'Net. The Junior Course is accessible from 1m20 (approximately age 6) and the Grand Course from 1m35 (approximately ages 9–10). Adult required depending on age and height.

Is parking free at Acro'Aventures Reignier?

Yes, parking at Acro'Aventures Reignier is free and located directly on site at the park entrance in the Rocailles forest.

What should I wear for Acro'Aventures Reignier?

For Acro'Aventures Reignier, wear sportswear and closed shoes that must cover heel and toes. Crocs, flip-flops and sandals are prohibited. Secure long hair and glasses, remove scarves and jewelry. Equipment (harness, pulley) is provided.

Can we come as a group to Acro'Aventures Reignier?

Yes, Acro'Aventures Reignier welcomes groups (schools, companies, birthdays, bachelor/bachelorette parties) with reduced rates from 8 people. Children's birthday packages with cake and activities available by booking.

Is there a maximum age for treetop courses at Reignier?

No, there is no maximum age. Courses are adapted according to height and physical condition. The Grand Course requires good fitness, while the Acro'Net suits all ages without harness.
