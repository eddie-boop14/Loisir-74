What is Thollon-les-Mémises

Thollon-les-Mémises is a small village resort in the Pays d'Évian, perched at 1 000 m above Lake Léman, about a dozen kilometres from Évian-les-Bains. A cable car (Poma Multix) links the village to the Mémises plateau and Pic des Mémises, between 1 400 and 1 974 m, with a renowned dual panorama over Lake Léman on one side and the Alps on the other.

The alpine ski area offers 16 slopes over 20 km, suited to beginners and intermediates. The lift pass is shared with the neighbouring Abondance/Essert area under the name Montagnes d'Évian, without the combined area's total being presented here as belonging to this resort alone.

On the cross-country side, Thollon-les-Mémises offers cross-country skiing as well as snowshoeing and ski touring routes (Pic des Mémises, Pic Boré, Pointe de la Pétalouse); the cross-country distance is not specified by official sources. The plateau is also home to a play area (Ludo Park), a toboggan run and a timed course, the Dré-Speed.

Awarded the Station Verte label, Thollon-les-Mémises remains a family-friendly, human-scale resort. In summer, the cable car keeps running to the Pas du Léman viewpoint (180° view over the lake and Switzerland), a starting point for hiking, paragliding and electrically-assisted mountain biking — a short distance from the off-slope activities along the shores of Lake Léman, on the Évian side.

Access

How to get there

By car

From Évian-les-Bains (about 12 km), follow the mountain road to Thollon-les-Mémises; allow about 22 km from Thonon-les-Bains and around fifty kilometres from Genève airport.

Without a car, a regular shuttle links Évian-les-Bains to Thollon-les-Mémises (about a 20-minute journey). The nearest station is Évian-les-Bains, from where shuttles depart for the resort.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does the Thollon-les-Mémises pass give access to other ski areas?

Yes. The pass is shared with the Abondance/Essert area under the name Montagnes d'Évian, in addition to the 16 slopes and 20 km of Thollon-les-Mémises' own ski area.

How can I reach Thollon-les-Mémises without a car?

A regular shuttle links Évian-les-Bains to Thollon-les-Mémises in about 20 minutes. The nearest station is Évian-les-Bains, 12 km from the resort.

Is there cross-country skiing or snowshoeing in Thollon-les-Mémises?

Yes, the resort offers cross-country skiing as well as snowshoeing and ski touring routes towards Pic des Mémises, Pic Boré and Pointe de la Pétalouse. The precise cross-country distance is not communicated by official sources.

What is there to do in Thollon-les-Mémises besides skiing?

The plateau offers Ludo Park, a toboggan run, the timed Dré-Speed course and, in summer, the Pas du Léman viewpoint, hiking, paragliding and e-mountain biking. A dozen kilometres away, on the Évian side, the Thermes d'Évian, the Palais Lumière, the Évian Resort casino and the CGN stop round out a family stay.
