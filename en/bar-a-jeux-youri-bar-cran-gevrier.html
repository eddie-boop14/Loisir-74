What is Le Youri Bar

Youri Game Bar is a board-game bar in Cran-Gevrier (part of Annecy). The venue offers 200+ games for all tastes: great classics, family games, party games, strategy, cooperation, ambient games. A trained host advises on the right game for your group, explains the rules and can play along. The atmosphere is cosy and friendly, with a bar and light food.

Who is it for? The concept suits families with children (selection from age 5-6) as well as friend groups, stag/hen, birthdays (children, teens, adults), after-work drinks and team-building. Access to the game library is a small flat fee (~€3-5/person) giving unlimited play all evening. Drinks (beer, wine, softs, snacks, boards) are extra.

Good to know. Booking is strongly recommended Friday evenings, weekends and for groups/birthdays. The venue is in Cran-Gevrier, reachable by Sibra bus from central Annecy and by bike. Neighbourhood parking nearby. For children's birthdays or stag/hen, tailored packages are offered with possible private area. Audience from age 5-6 for family games, no upper limit.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much for the games?

1 drink/person/hour for 100+ games. €3/person/hour if you don't drink.

Do I need to book?

Not in the week, recommended on weekends. Groups/private hire: contact the bar.

Is there food?

Yes, Savoie meat/cheese/sausage boards.

Can families come?

Yes, family-friendly early evening; kid-friendly game suggestions.

Can I private hire?

Yes, Thursday-Monday up to 100. Great for birthdays, bachelor/ette parties, seminars.

Where do I park?

Free at the Verts Prés business park, Cran-Gevrier.

Is it next to the escape game?

Yes, next to L'Atelier des Énigmes — perfect combo.
