Écomusée de la pêche et du lac

The only museum with aquariums in Haute-Savoie: 3 traditional fishermen's huts at the Port de Rives, 2 aquariums of Lake Geneva species, ornithological collection.

TypeMunicipal ecomuseum, 3 traditional 12th-c. fishermen's huts
AccessPaid (gratuit -18 ans et tous les dimanches)
TownThonon-les-Bains
Duration45 min to 1 hr 30
ParkingAristide Briand car park 620 m away

PramMain hut accessible with help (step)
Best season30 May — 30 September · daily July-August

Qu'est-ce que Écomusée de la pêche et du lac ?

The Écomusée de la pêche et du lac is the only public site with aquariums in Haute-Savoie (confirmed by the Haute-Savoie Tourist Office: 'No aquarium in the Haute-Savoie département' — the Thonon ecomuseum is the sole exception). A municipal museum of the Town of Thonon-les-Bains created in 1987, it is housed in 3 traditional fishermen's huts at the Port de Rives, a historic fishermen's quarter going back to the 12th century, recognisable by its Tour des Langues.

Contents: 2 aquariums of Lake Geneva species (perch, féra, Arctic char, burbot, trout, carp, roach, Californian crayfish); naturalised ornithological collection (lake birds); traditional fishing equipment (boats, fish traps, nets, old and modern engines); the Paul Perroud hut preserved as is, an in-situ workshop of a mid-20th-century fisherman; documentary film about contemporary professional fishing on Lake Geneva; PegaseLAB app (free download on Apple/Google Play) for teenagers and adults — an investigation into the poisoning of fisherman Jim.

ALERT as of 23 May 2026: MUSEUM CLOSED. Seasonal reopening: 30 May 2026 (Fête du Nautisme weekend), until 30 September 2026. Very accessible pricing: adult €3.50, reduced rate €2.60, free under 18, free for everyone every Sunday. Combined ticket with the Musée du Chablais (Château de Sonnaz, same Town of Thonon): full €6 / reduced €4 for both municipal museums.

Frequently asked questions

How do I book at Écomusée de la pêche et du lac?
Écomusée de la pêche et du lac operates on open access during the summer season. Ticketing on site at the entrance to the huts. Group bookings by phone +33 (0)4 50 70 26 96 or email musees@ville-thonon.fr.
What is the price of Écomusée de la pêche et du lac?
Écomusée de la pêche et du lac: full adult rate €3.50, reduced rate €2.60. FREE under 18 and ALL Sundays. Combined ticket Musée du Chablais + Écomusée full €6 / reduced €4. Group €2.60. Verified on 23 May 2026.
What are the opening hours of Écomusée de la pêche et du lac?
Écomusée de la pêche et du lac: season 30 May – 30 Sept 2026. May/June/Sept Wed-Sun 2.30pm-6pm. July-August daily 10am-12.30pm and 2.30pm-6pm. Closed in winter. Verified on the official site on 23 May 2026 — subject to seasonal changes.
Is there an aquarium at Écomusée de la pêche et du lac?
Yes. Écomusée de la pêche et du lac presents 2 aquariums of Lake Geneva species (perch, féra, Arctic char, burbot, trout, carp, roach, Californian crayfish). It is the ONLY public site with aquariums in Haute-Savoie.
Is Écomusée de la pêche et du lac free on Sundays?
Yes. Écomusée de la pêche et du lac is FREE for EVERYONE EVERY Sunday of the season (30 May – 30 Sept 2026). Exceptional Town of Thonon policy (same policy as the Musée du Chablais at Château de Sonnaz).
Combined ticket Écomusée de la pêche et du lac and Musée du Chablais?
Yes. A combined ticket pairs Écomusée de la pêche et du lac with the Musée du Chablais (Château de Sonnaz): full €6 / reduced €4 for both municipal museums of the Town of Thonon-les-Bains. Saving compared to separate tickets.
Is Écomusée de la pêche et du lac accessible for visitors with reduced mobility?
Partly. At Écomusée de la pêche et du lac, the main hut ground floor (aquariums) is accessible with help (entrance step). 1st floor by stairs only. Audiovisual hut difficult (gravel approach). Reduced rate €2.60 for visitors with disabilities.
How do I get to Écomusée de la pêche et du lac from Évian?
Écomusée de la pêche et du lac is 15 min from Évian via the N5 and central Thonon. By public transport: Léman Express L1 or TER L3 to Thonon SNCF station. Funicular upper town↔Port de Rives. Seasonal CGN Évian-Thonon boats. Aristide Briand car park 620 m away.
