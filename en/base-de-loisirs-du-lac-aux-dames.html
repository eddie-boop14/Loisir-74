What is Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames

The Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames is a 10-hectare leisure park located in Samoëns, built around two adjoining lakes. Away from the village centre, it offers a calm and secure environment, free from vehicle traffic, ideal for families and groups of friends. The base is open year-round and accessible free of charge.

The site offers a wide variety of activities: supervised swimming in the lake from early July to early September (12pm–6pm), children's playground, sports fields (football, basketball, beach volleyball, pétanque), fishing, walks around the lakes, stand-up paddling, discovery trail. Paid facilities are also available: open-air swimming pool with water slides and three pools, tennis and padel courts, adventure course, paragliding.

The leisure base incorporates Espace Le Bois aux Dames, a cultural and sports centre with performance hall (426 seated, 1,100 standing), which hosts concerts, theatre productions and sporting events. Two bars-restaurants and picnic areas with sanitary facilities complete the offering. Starting point for family hikes to the Giffre riverside and inter-lakes trail to Morillon.

Access

How to get there

By car

From Cluses (20 km, 25 min), take the D907 towards Taninges then Samoëns. At the entrance to Samoëns, follow signs for 'Base de loisirs' or 'Lac aux Dames'. Free parking on site. From Geneva (60 km, 1 hour), A40 exit 18 Cluses, then D907.

Open in Maps

By bike

Access possible via the Giffre Green Way (family trail following the Giffre river from Morillon). Secure itinerary, accessible to bikes and mountain bikes. From Samoëns town centre, 1.5 km by road or footpath.

When to visit

When to visit

The leisure base is accessible year-round. Summer (May to September) is peak season: supervised swimming in July–August, open-air pool open, all activities available, animated terraces. Spring and autumn are quieter, ideal for walks, fishing and land-based activities. In winter, the site is regularly snow-covered and the lakes frozen: the base becomes a starting point for snowshoe walks and cross-country skiing along the Giffre. Playgrounds and sports fields remain accessible when snow-free. Espace Le Bois aux Dames hosts cultural events year-round.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is entry to Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames free?

Yes. Entry to Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames is free year-round. Some activities on site are paid: open-air swimming pool, tennis, adventure course, paddle rental.

Is swimming allowed in the lakes at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames?

Yes. Swimming is supervised in one of the two lakes at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames from July 6 to September 1, 12pm to 6pm. Outside the supervision period, swimming is possible but unsupervised.

Where can I park at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames?

Multiple free parking areas are available directly at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames. Sufficient capacity even during peak season. Access also possible on foot from Samoëns town centre (1.5 km).

Are dogs allowed at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames?

Yes, dogs are allowed on leash at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames. Specific regulations apply (municipal by-law available on the Samoëns town hall website).

Is Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames is wheelchair accessible with assistance and independently. The site has PMR spaces, adapted toilets with grab bars and seating areas. The trail around the lakes is practicable.

Can I picnic at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames?

Yes. Several picnic areas equipped with tables, benches, bins and sanitary facilities are available free of charge at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames. Shaded setting between the two lakes.

Are there restaurants at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames?

Yes. Two bars-restaurants are present at Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames: L'Entre 2 Lacs and the Espace Le Bois aux Dames bar. Quick service, snacks, drinks. Terraces with lake views.

Is Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames open in winter?

Yes. Base de loisirs du Lac aux Dames is open year-round. In winter, the site is often snow-covered and the lakes frozen. Starting point for snowshoe walks and cross-country skiing along the Giffre. Playgrounds remain accessible.
