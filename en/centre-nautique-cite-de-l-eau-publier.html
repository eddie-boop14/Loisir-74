What is Cité de l'Eau Aquatic Centre — Publier

The Cité de l'Eau aquatic centre in Publier-Amphion is the inter-municipal water complex of the Pays d'Évian Vallée d'Abondance community. Set just steps from Lake Geneva, it offers more than 4,500 m² of facilities: a 25m six-lane sports pool, a fun pool, a paddling pool for toddlers, slides, a current river and a wellness area with sauna and hammam. It is one of the main indoor aquatic facilities of the Évian area, used by both locals and holidaymakers.

Activities and audiences. The centre welcomes serious swimmers (lanes reserved early morning and at certain times), families with children (fun area, paddling pool, slides), seniors (aquagym, dedicated slots) and school groups. Programme of coached activities: aquagym, aquabike, swimming school, parent-and-baby classes, water garden. The wellness area (sauna, hammam, sensory showers) is available with an extra fee and open to over-16s. Lifeguards are present at all times around the pools.

Good to know. The centre is open year-round, with extended hours during school holidays. Public prices are around €5-8 for adults and €3-5 for children; 10-entry cards, monthly/annual passes and reduced rates are available. Swim caps compulsory, soapy shower before swimming. Free parking, bus access from Évian and Thonon. Perfect for an indoor activity alongside a day by the lake.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What pools are available?

The centre has a 25m six-lane sports pool, a fun pool with current river and massage jets, a paddling pool for toddlers and water slides.

How much is admission?

Adult admission is around €5-8, child €3-5, free for under-4s. The wellness area (sauna/hammam) is extra. 10-entry cards and annual passes available. 2026 prices to confirm on the website.

Are swim caps required?

Yes, swim caps are compulsory in all pools. They can be bought at reception. A soapy shower before swimming is also compulsory.

Is there a wellness area?

Yes, the centre has a wellness area with sauna, hammam and sensory showers, available with an extra fee. Reserved for teens and adults from age 16.

Can I enrol my children in lessons?

Yes, the swim school offers group or private lessons for children and adults (beginners, improvers). Baby swim and water garden for the youngest. Online or on-site enrolment.

Is the centre wheelchair-accessible?

Yes, the facility is wheelchair-accessible: equipped changing rooms and pool access. A pool hoist is available on request.

How to get there without a car?

The centre is served by CCPEVA bus lines from Évian and Thonon. The Lake Geneva cycle greenway runs nearby — ideal by bike from Évian-Amphion.
