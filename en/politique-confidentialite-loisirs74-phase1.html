Data protection

Privacy Policy

What we do — and do not do — with your data on loisirs74.fr.

← Back to home

The essentials in 5 points

  • No form, no account, no booking on the site.
  • No advertising tracker, no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel.
  • If you send us a photo, you authorise us to use it with credit to your name — you can request removal at any time.
  • "Partner" establishments are featured free of charge; no paid service is marketed to date.
  • Request to access, modify or delete your data: contact@loisirs74.fr

🏢 Data controller

The controller of the personal data collected on loisirs74.fr is Eddie Raset, publisher of the site as a sole trader.

Contact for any GDPR question: contact@loisirs74.fr

Given the size of the activity and the limited nature of the data collected, no Data Protection Officer (DPO) has been appointed. Requests are handled directly by the publisher.

📋 What data we collect

a · When you browse

The site collects no personal data on visitors who simply consult the pages. No registration form. No Google Analytics, Matomo, Hotjar or other audience-measurement tool.

b · When you write to us

If you use the mailto: links on the site to contact us, you naturally transmit: your email, your name (if visible) and the content of your message. These exchanges are processed on the basis of legitimate interest (Article 6.1.f GDPR) or pre-contractual performance if your request concerns a partnership (Article 6.1.b).

c · When you submit photos

If you send photos to photos@loisirs74.fr or post on Instagram with the hashtag #loisirs74, we collect: your photos, your name and/or your Instagram handle (to credit you) and the email address if you send by email. Submission constitutes authorisation for use on the site with credit.

d · When you propose a partnership

If you contact us to propose your establishment as a free "Partner", we collect the information needed: name of the establishment, name of the manager, email, possible phone number, website. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Article 6.1.f GDPR) in evaluating your request.

e · Technical host data

Our host Netlify, Inc. automatically logs, for the operation of the service: IP address, date/time of requests, pages visited. These logs are kept according to Netlify's policy.

💾 Local storage in your browser

The site uses two storage areas in your browser — neither cookies nor data sent to our servers:

localStorage (theme)
Light/dark theme preference, if you use the button.

This storage is exempt from consent as it is strictly necessary for the functionality you have activated. You can clear it at any time in your browser settings.

🍪 Cookies and trackers

Loisirs 74 places no advertising cookies, no audience-measurement cookies, no social-network cookies.

No consent banner is therefore necessary. If we were to add one in the future (statistics tool, video embed), a CNIL-compliant banner would be put in place at that time, with accept and refuse buttons of equal prominence.

💰 Commercial partnerships and transparency

Loisirs 74 currently offers local establishments only free placements ("Partner"). In accordance with Article L121-1 of the French Consumer Code and DGCCRF guidelines on advertising transparency:

  • "Partner" inserts are clearly identified as such by a visible badge.
  • The main editorial content of the pages (description, facts, sources) is never modified based on the presence of a partner.
  • No payment is received to modify, remove or bias factual information.
  • No paid placement is currently marketed. If this changes, the privacy policy and legal notice will be updated, and General Terms and Conditions of Sale will be published.

⚖️ Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR (Articles 15 to 22) and the French Data Protection Act, you have:

  • A right of access to your data
  • A right of rectification
  • A right of erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • A right to object to processing
  • A right to restriction of processing
  • A right to portability
  • A right to set post-mortem instructions

To exercise these rights: contact@loisirs74.fr. We respond within one month maximum. In case of disagreement, you may file a complaint with the CNIL — www.cnil.fr.

📸 Specific rights of photo contributors

If you have submitted photos via email or Instagram hashtag, you retain your copyright over these works. You can at any time:

  • Request the removal of your photos from the site
  • Change the credit displayed (name, handle)
  • Receive a copy of the photos we use

Write to photos@loisirs74.fr. Removal takes effect within 7 days.

🔐 Security

The site is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption) on all pages. Professional email accounts are protected with two-factor authentication.

In the event of a security incident affecting your data, we would inform you within the GDPR deadlines (72 hours to notify the CNIL if there is a proven risk).

👶 Children and minors

The site is not specifically aimed at minors under 15 and is not intended to collect their data. If you are a parent and believe that your child has transmitted information to us without your consent, contact us — we will proceed with deletion.

✏️ Changes

This policy may change, in particular if new services are added (newsletter, statistics tool, booking). The date of the last update is indicated at the bottom of the page.
