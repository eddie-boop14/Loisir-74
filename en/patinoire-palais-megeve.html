What is Patinoire du Palais Megève

The Patinoire du Palais Megève is part of the Le Palais des Sports complex in Megève, a flagship facility of this prestigious ski resort founded by the Rothschilds. An Olympic-size ice rink complemented by a swimming pool and a fitness area, it hosts public sessions, lessons, hockey matches and galas throughout the winter season.

The rink welcomes the Mont-Blanc Hockey Club for home games, as well as figure skating galas and regional championships, with a packed events calendar from December to March. Public sessions alternate with sports activities, making the rink a key family destination of the resort.

2026 prices: adult 6 EUR, child 4.50 EUR. Season: mainly December to March. Hours: winter school holidays daily 2-6pm and 8-10pm; outside holidays, weekends only. Step-free access available.
