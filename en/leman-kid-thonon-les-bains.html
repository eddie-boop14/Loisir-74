About Léman Kid

Léman Kid is an indoor play park for children aged 0 to 12, at Impasse de la Poudrière in Thonon-les-Bains. The park is organised into 3 dedicated age zones (0-2, 3-5, 4-12), heated and air-conditioned, with a mini electric karting track, multi-level structures, a motor-skills circuit and private birthday-party rooms.

Clear unlimited-entry pricing: free for under 1s (accompanying adult +€1), €7 for ages 1-3, €10 for ages 3-12. Accompanying adult costs an extra €1 according to the venue's pricing page. The Pass Family 10-entry pass valid 2 years (€60 for under 3s, €90 for 3+) includes 2 free adults and €1 karting tokens. Birthday parties in 2-hour packages at €18 per child (4 to 8 children, 2 adults free, morning / 2 pm / 2.30 pm slots).

A savoury snack is served from 11 am to 1 pm (orders until 12.45 pm). Outside food and drinks are not allowed except specific baby food, bottles (microwave available), water and fruit. Socks required for parents and children (on sale at reception). ALERT: exceptional closure Saturday 23 and Sunday 24 May 2026 for the Pentecost weekend.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I book at Léman Kid?

Léman Kid is free entry during opening hours, without booking. Booking required for birthday parties (morning / 2 pm / 2.30 pm slots) online at lemankid.com or by phone on +33 7 44 40 87 44.

How much does Léman Kid cost?

Léman Kid: under 1 free (accompanying adult +€1), 1-3 €7, 3-12 €10, extra accompanying adult +€1, 12+ free. Pass Family 10 entries €60 (under 3) or €90 (3+). Birthday €18/child. Verified 21 May 2026.

What are the opening hours of Léman Kid?

Léman Kid: outside holidays Wednesday, Saturday, Sunday 10 am – 6 pm. Zone A school holidays and public holidays Tuesday-Sunday 10 am – 6 pm. Savoury snack 11 am – 1 pm. CLOSED 23-24 May 2026 (Pentecost). Verified on the official website on 21 May 2026 — subject to seasonal changes.

What is the minimum age at Léman Kid?

Léman Kid welcomes children from birth to age 12 in 3 dedicated age zones: 0-2 (babies), 3-5 (preschool), 4-12 (older). Free under 1 and over 12 (accompanying). Heated and air-conditioned premises.

Are socks required at Léman Kid?

Yes. Socks ARE REQUIRED at Léman Kid for parents and children (on sale at reception if you forget). Bare feet and shoes are not allowed on the mats and play structures for hygiene and safety.

Can you eat at Léman Kid?

Léman Kid offers a savoury snack on site between 11 am and 1 pm (orders until 12.45 pm). Outside food and drinks are not allowed, except specific baby food, bottles (microwave available), water and fruit.

How much does a birthday party at Léman Kid cost?

Léman Kid birthday: €18/child for 2 hours, morning / 2 pm / 2.30 pm slots, 4 to 8 children per package, 2 adults included free. Private room, hosting; cake and snack optional. Book online at lemankid.com.

How do I get to Léman Kid from Évian?

Léman Kid is 15 min from Évian-les-Bains via the N5 and Thonon bypass, exit 2 Armoy/Bellevaux then signs for Thonon-les-Bains. By public transport: Léman Express L2 to Thonon SNCF station (3.5 km) then BUT! bus. Free on-site parking.

How much is entry for a child?

Between €7 and €10 (ages 2-12) depending on conditions. Free for under 1s.
