Frequently asked questions

What to do in Annecy when it rains, with the family?

The Animation Film Museum, La Turbine sciences (interactive centre), a cinema screening, bowling or the indoor pool of l'Île Bleue are perfect with children on a rainy day.

Which museums to visit in Annecy in the rain?

The Musée-Château d'Annecy, the Palais de l'Île, the Animation Film Museum, the lake Écomusée in Sévrier and the Fonderie Paccard — all under cover.

Where to have fun indoors near Annecy?

Escape game (La Grande Évasion), laser game in Sillingy, the Space Bloc climbing gym, bowling in Annecy or Sévrier, and the Jean-Régis ice rink.

Is there an indoor pool in Annecy?

Yes: the Jean-Régis pool in Annecy and the l'Île Bleue aquatic centre in Seynod welcome swimmers all year round.
