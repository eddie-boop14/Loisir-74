Frequently asked questions

Which beaches on Lake Annecy are really free?

4 beaches are completely free (free entry and parking). 4 others have free entry but paid parking in season. 3 charge admission in summer.

Which beaches charge an entry fee?

Three, in summer only: Saint-Jorioz (from 2,60 €), Menthon-Saint-Bernard (4,60 € adult) and the Impérial in Annecy (2026 price to be confirmed). All are free off-season.

Watch out for parking: where do you have to pay?

Several free beaches have paid parking in season: Sévrier, Duingt, Doussard and Angon. The real cost of a day out isn't always where you'd expect.

Which beach for watersports?

Saint-Jorioz, with its watersports club (sailing, paddle, wingfoil), is the most activity-focused.
