# Plage de Saint-Jorioz

## Facts

- Commune: Saint-Jorioz, Haute-Savoie (74410)
- GPS: 45.8335, 6.1565
- Category: lac
- Type: Developed beach · Natural sand

## Hours

1 January – 30 June: free access daily. 1 July – 31 August: paid access 9:30 to 17:30 (lifeguard supervised). Closed 7:00–9:30 for cleaning. 1 September – 31 December: free access daily. Verified on official website 14 May 2026 — subject to seasonal changes.
